Class Time Policy



Students will be working until the class dismisses. NO FREE TIME.

Online teaching and learning will be new to all of us, but this doesn't mean we are not here to learn.



1. First part of class (approx. 20 mins) will be Synchronous learning. This means the teacher will be leading the instruction in real-time.


2. Second part of the class (approx. 40 mins) will be Asynchronous learning. This means you will be responsible for your own learning. This will either be independent practice or small-group work.


3. You must have your camera on during both.

Electronic Devices



Unless the device is being used for work in my class, you must turn them off during class time.


1. First Offense: Student will be reminded of class policy.


2. Second Offense: Parent phone call or email.


3. Third Offense: Disciplinary action will be discussed with the 7th Grade Principal (Mr. Bui).

Absences and Grades



1. Grades for weekly and daily work will be updated by Friday, every week (DO-NOW, participation, and quizzes). Test grades will be updated within 24 hours AFTER ALL STUDENTS have completed the test.


2. Please check grades regularly and feel free to ask me any questions that you might have about a particular grade.


3. If you are absent, you are responsible for completing your make-up work (One day to make up for each day absent. If you're absent 3 days, you will have 3 days to make up the work you missed.


4. If you know you're going to be absent beforehand, you should ask me for the work before the absence. That way you don't have to catch-up when you get back.


5. If you need help to complete the assignments you missed while you were absent, I can help you before or after school, or during my office hours.
